Creamy Garlic Mushroom Pasta

Creamy Garlic Mushroom Pasta is the ultimate comfort dish for mushroom lovers. Tender fettuccine noodles are coated in a rich, velvety garlic-infused cream sauce, tossed with golden sautéed mushrooms, and finished with a sprinkle of parmesan and fresh parsley. Each bite is a perfect harmony of earthy, savory, and buttery flavors — making it a quick yet indulgent meal that feels like a warm hug in a bowl. Whether you’re cooking for one or flexing in front of guests, this dish never fails to impress.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti or fettuccine
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 200g mushrooms (sliced — any kind, but button or cremini slap)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Boil dat pasta — Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Sauté squad — Heat olive oil in a pan, add garlic, and sauté until fragrant (don’t burn it, it’s shy).
  3. Mushroom madness — Toss in mushrooms. Cook for 5–7 mins until golden brown and tender.
  4. Creamy dreams — Pour in heavy cream and let it simmer for 2 mins. Stir in parmesan and mix until creamy.
  5. Mix it all up — Add the cooked pasta to the pan. Toss it like you’re in a cooking show until it’s all coated.
  6. Final touches — Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with chopped parsley if you're feelin’ fancy.
  7. Serve — Serve hot and prepare for compliments ✨
